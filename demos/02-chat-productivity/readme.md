# Copilot Chat, Productivity Agents & Microsoft Frontier

Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat is the always-on AI you talk to in plain language to draft, summarize, and answer questions. This module covers the chat experience and the productivity capabilities around it: building no-code apps from a description, and putting prebuilt and site-grounded agents to work. It is split into three focused topics.

## Topics

| # | Topic | What you learn |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 1 | [Copilot Chat & Microsoft Frontier](./01-copilot-chat-frontier/readme.md) | The chat experience, free vs licensed Copilot, Copilot Tasks, and the Frontier early-access program |
| 2 | [Build No-Code Apps with App Builder](./02-app-builder/readme.md) | Turn a plain-language description into a working app with data, and how App Builder differs from agent building |
| 3 | [Productivity & SharePoint Agents](./03-productivity-sharepoint-agents/readme.md) | The prebuilt agents (Researcher, Analyst, and coaches) and SharePoint agents scoped to your sites and documents |
