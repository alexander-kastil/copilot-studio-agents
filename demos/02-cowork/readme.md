# Module 2: Microsoft 365 Copilot Cowork

> **Frontier Preview**: Cowork requires enrollment in the [Microsoft Frontier Program](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/frontier-program/). Features are subject to change.

Cowork is Microsoft 365's agentic AI that does work on your behalf. You describe an outcome in plain language, Cowork plans and executes the steps across your apps, and you approve before anything is sent or saved. This module is split into six focused topics: start with getting started and its core capabilities, learn to use and author skills, make that work recurring and extensible, then keep it all under human control.

## Topics

| # | Topic | What you learn |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 1 | [Getting Started with Cowork](./01-intro/readme.md) | What Cowork is, how it differs from Copilot Chat, where to access it, the interaction model, and its core capabilities across communication, documents, calendar, research, and automation |
| 2 | [Understanding Skills: Built-in Skills & the Side Panel](./02-built-in-skills/readme.md) | The built-in skill catalog, how the side panel shows Cowork's live work, and where additional skills come from |

| 4 | [Scheduling Prompts & Recurring Automation](./04-automation/readme.md) | Running a prompt or a custom skill on a recurring schedule while high-risk actions still pause for approval |
| 5 | [Extending Cowork with Plugins](./05-plugins/readme.md) | Installing plugins from the Microsoft 365 App Store and community repositories to add external data and expertise |
| 6 | [Human-in-the-Loop: Approving Actions & Staying in Control](./06-staying-in-control/readme.md) | The approval model, risk levels, pause and resume, feedback, and how admins govern Cowork |

## Licensing at a Glance

Cowork is metered by usage-based billing in Copilot Credits, not a per-user seat. The tenant needs at least one Microsoft 365 Copilot license, Frontier enrollment, and Anthropic enabled as an AI subprocessor. Setup and pay-as-you-go configuration are covered in [Module 1: Pay as you go licensing](../01-intro/05-licensing-setup/readme.md#pay-as-you-go-licensing).

## Hands-On Learning

Cowork is best understood by delegating real work and watching it plan, act, and pause for approval. These guided paths take you from your first request through authoring and packaging your own skills.
